High quality design
in just a few clicks

The Arch Viz Tool Kit will speed up your architectural visualization workflow.

Easy to use

Various templates allow you to create custom sized windows, doors, and mouldings with just a few clicks.

Boost your productivity

The tool will automatically calculate the boundaries of your cut-out, creating the object and a light mass portal, for beautiful renderings.

Quick revisions

The Arch Viz Tool Kit is designed to instantly get results where it matters, allowing you to update sets of objects with just one click. No more adjusting meshes and having to deal with UVs for your windows, doors, and mouldings.

Animation friendly

Windows and doors can be tilted and turned in the editor, as well as on run time. Giving you full control to animate them in sequences, virtual reality, or in games, at all times.

Milestones

The Arch Viz Tool Kit is currently in the prototyping phase. The production is divided into the following milestones:

  • Funding
    Securing funding for the remaining milestones.
  • Milestone 1 - Further prototyping
    I am planning to dedicate the first 8 weeks to improving the current prototype, as well as designing a user interface, which seamlessly integrates into the existing Unreal Engine tool palette.
  • Milestone 2 - Research
    Research about the most popular styles in architectural visualizations will be the foundation for the next milestone. I am hoping to quickly gather data, together with the University of Applied Arts and Sciences Hannover, to then decide on a number of styles and materials as references for the next step.
  • Milestone 3 - 3D Modeling
    This milestone will require designing high quality modular assets in the previously determined styles. This might also require adjusting features of the plugin, so various details (such as position of hinges, etc.) can be taken into consideration by the system.
  • Milestone 4 - Testing
    In this step I plan on reaching out to design agencies, which specialize in architectural visualizations, to thoroughly test the product.
  • Milestone 5 - Optimization & Finalization
    After gathering data from testing with design agencies and architects, further adjustments, bug fixes, and feature implementations will be done, to finalize the product.

    The finalized products aim to include a minimum of 5 sets of modular assets for each tool, as well as a number of different materials/colors to choose from, for each set.
  • Milestone 6 - ShippingArch Viz Tool Kit.
